Dataset consists of 60 video fragments that have been viewed and evaluated by two group of people:

  • First group - 302 people (denotes as p-13)
  • Second group - 10 people (denotes as 702)

p-13 folder consists of:

  • File with viewing order (direct and backward)
  • 60 subfolders, each contains video fragment, it's grade and information about distortions in the video

702 folder has the same structure except of video fragments itself, because they duplicate fragments of p-13
