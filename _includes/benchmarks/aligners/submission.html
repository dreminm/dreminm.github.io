There are 3 easy steps to submit:

  1. Download dataset here.
    The directory tree and it's description is inside the zip-file

  2. Test your algorithm

  3. Send us an email to aligners-benchmark@videoprocessing.ai with the following information:
      A. Name of your method that will be specified in our benchmark

      B. Link to the cloud drive (Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, etc.), containing the output of your algorithm

      C. (Optional) Any additional information about the method
      Click here to see what may be included in additional information
        The name of the theoretical method used
        Full name of your method or product
        The version that was used
        Any other additional information
        A link to the code of your method, if it is open-source
        A link to the paper about your method
        A link to the page, where users can purchase or download your product (for example, VirtualDub Plugin)
