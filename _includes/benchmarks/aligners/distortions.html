  • Inserting a fragment (randomly selects a video from the dataset, randomly cuts it to ⅔ the original length, and inserts it in a random place of the distorted video)
  • Missing fragment (randomly select the length of the fragment <⅓ of the entire video and the location from which it will disappear)
  • Crop (randomly from ⅔ to ⅓ of the original length) + shift up to 250 frames from the initial frame
